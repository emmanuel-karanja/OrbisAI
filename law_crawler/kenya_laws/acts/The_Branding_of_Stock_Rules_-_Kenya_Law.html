LAWS OF KENYA

BRANDING OF STOCK ACT

THE BRANDING OF STOCK RULES

GAZETTE NOTICE 1223 OF 1950

  • Published in Kenya Gazette Vol. LII—No. 54 on 7 November 1950
  • Commenced on 7 November 1950
  1. [Amended by The Branding of Stock (Amendment) Rules, 1951 (Gazette Notice 766 of 1951) on 3 July 1951]
  2. [Amended by The Branding of Stock (Amendment) Rules, 1964 (Legal Notice 39 of 1964) on 28 January 1964]
  3. [Amended by The Branding of Stock (Amendment) Rules, 1965 (Legal Notice 224 of 1965) on 24 August 1965]
  4. [Amended by The Branding of Stock (Amendment) Rules, 1966 (Legal Notice 327 of 1966) on 29 November 1966]
  5. [Amended by The Branding of Stock (Amendment) Rules, 1967 (Legal Notice 152 of 1967) on 1 January 1967]
  6. [Amended by The Branding of Stock (Amendment) Rules, 1973 (Legal Notice 124 of 1973) on 13 July 1973]
  7. [Amended by The Branding of Stock (Amendment) Rules, 1980 (Legal Notice 87 of 1980) on 13 June 1980]
  8. [Amended by The Branding of Stock (Amendment) Rules, 1995 (Legal Notice 274 of 1995) on 28 July 1995]
  9. [Revised by 24th Annual Supplement (Legal Notice 221 of 2023) on 31 December 2022]
1.These Rules may be cited as the Branding of Stock Rules.
2.Branding-irons and branding instruments shall only be procurable through the Registrar of Brands, c/o the Veterinary Department, P.O. Kabete, Nairobi, to whom all applications shall be addressed.
3.The persons named in the Schedule to these Rules are authorised to manufacture branding-irons and branding instruments for sale at the respective prices specified in the said Schedule.
