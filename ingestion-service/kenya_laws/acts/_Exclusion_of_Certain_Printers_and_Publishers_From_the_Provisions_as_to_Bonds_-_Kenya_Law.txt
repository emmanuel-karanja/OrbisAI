LAWS OF KENYA




BOOKS AND NEWSPAPERS ACT


EXCLUSION OF CERTAIN PRINTERS AND PUBLISHERS FROM THE PROVISIONS AS TO BONDS


LEGAL NOTICE 81 OF 2009





      
        
          Published in Kenya Gazette on 5 June 2009
        
      
    



          Commenced on 5 June 2009
          
        







    
      
      
        
          [Revised by 
24th Annual Supplement (Legal Notice 221 of 2023)
 on 
31 December 2022
]
        
      
    
  






In exercise of the powers conferred by section 10(2) of the Books and Newspapers Act (Cap. 111), the Attorney-General excludes all printers and publishers from the provisions of Part III of the Act (which deals with bonds) in respect of the newspaper “PR Week Report”.
