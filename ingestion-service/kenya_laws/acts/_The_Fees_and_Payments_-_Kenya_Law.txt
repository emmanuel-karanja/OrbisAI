LAWS OF KENYA




ANIMAL DISEASES ACT


THE FEES AND PAYMENTS


LEGAL NOTICE 266 OF 2021





      
        
          
            Published in Kenya Gazette Vol. CXXIV—No. 6 on 14 January 2022
          
        
      
    



          Commenced on 31 December 2021
          
        







    
      
      
        
          [Revised by 
24th Annual Supplement (Legal Notice 221 of 2023)
 on 
31 December 2022
]
        
      
    
  






IN EXERCISE of the powers conferred by section 15 of the Animal Diseases Act, the Director of Veterinary Services amends the Fees and Payments prescribed under the said section by deleting parts 1(b) and 1(d) of the First Schedule and substituting therefor the parts set out in the Schedule.
L.N. No. 166 of 2021 is revoked.
