LAWS OF KENYA




ADVOCATES ACT


THE ADVOCATES (PRACTISING CERTIFICATE) (FEES) RULES


LEGAL NOTICE 110 OF 2024





      
        
          
            Published in Kenya Gazette Vol. CXXVI—No. 108 on 19 July 2024
          
        
      
    



          Commenced on 15 July 2024
          
        






1. Citation
These Rules may be cited as the Advocates (Practising Certificate) (Fees) Rules, 2024.
2. Fees for certificated under section 26 of Advocates Act
The fees payable for a practising certificate issued to an advocate under section 26(2) of the Advocates Act shall be a specified in the Schedule hereto.
3. Remittance of fees
The fees payable under these Rules shall be paid to the Society in accordance with the provisions of section 84 of the Advocates Act.
4. Revocation of L.N. No. 193 of 2003
The Advocates (Practising Certificate) (Fees) Rules, 2002 are revoked.
