LAWS OF KENYA




BROKERS ACT


THE BROKERS (LICENCE FEES) ORDER


LEGAL NOTICE 84 OF 1981





      
        
          
            Published in Kenya Gazette Vol. LXXXIII—No. 26 on 19 June 1981
          
        
      
    



          Commenced on 19 June 1981
          
        







    
      
      
        
          [Amended by 
Finance Act, 1986 (Act No. 10 of 1986)
 on 
13 June 1981
]
        
      
    
  



    
      
      
        
          [Amended by 
The Brokers (Licence Fees) Order, 1989 (Legal Notice 200 of 1989)
 on 
23 June 1989
]
        
      
    
  



    
      
      
        
          [Revised by 
24th Annual Supplement (Legal Notice 221 of 2023)
 on 
31 December 2022
]
        
      
    
  






1.
This Order may be cited as the Brokers (Licence Fees) Order.
2.
The fees payable under section 3 of the Act for licences in respect of the trades specified in the first column of the Schedule to this Order shall be the fees respectively specified in the second column thereof.
SCHEDULE 
[para. 2]
Fees
 
Sh.
Ordinary broker..............................................................................................
600

1,800
