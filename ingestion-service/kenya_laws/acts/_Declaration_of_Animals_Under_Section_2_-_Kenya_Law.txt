Declaration of Animals under Section 2
IN EXERCISE of the powers conferred by section 2 of the Animal Diseases Act, the Minister for Livestock and Fisheries Development declares the following to be animals for the purpose of the Act:—
Fish and Bees
[
L.N. 158/2007
.]
