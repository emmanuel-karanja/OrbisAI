LAWS OF KENYA




BIRTHS AND DEATHS REGISTRATION ACT


THE BIRTHS AND DEATHS REGISTRATION ACT - DECLARATION


LEGAL NOTICE 151 OF 2024





      
        
          
            Published in Kenya Gazette Vol. CXXVI—No. 167 on 11 October 2024
          
        
      
    



          Commenced on 1 October 2024
          
        






IN EXERCISE of the powers conferred by section 9(2) and 15(2) of the Births and Deaths Registration Act, the Cabinet Secretary for Interior and National Administration declares that the registration of the births and deaths of all inhabitants of the remaining non-compulsory areas within the Republic of Kenya is compulsory with effect from 1
st
 September, 1971.
