LAWS OF KENYA




BANKING ACT


BANKING ACT - EXEMPTION


LEGAL NOTICE 52 OF 2024





      
        
          
            Published in Kenya Gazette Vol. CXXVI—No. 48 on 12 April 2024
          
        
      
    



          Commenced on 11 March 2024
          
        






IN EXERCISE of the powers conferred by section 53(1) of the Banking Act, the Cabinet Secretary for the National Treasury and Economic Planning exempts Absa Bank Kenya Plc from the provisions of section 12(a) and 12(c) of the Banking Act, for a period not exceeding five years with effect from the 1st February, 2024.
