LAWS OF KENYA




AGRICULTURAL FINANCE CORPORATION ACT


THE DETERMINATION OF LIMIT OF TOTAL INDEBTEDNESS


LEGAL NOTICE 256 OF 1992





      
        
          
            Published in Kenya Gazette Vol. XCIV—No. 46 on 9 October 1992
          
        
      
    



          Commenced on 9 October 1992
          
        







    
      
      
        
          [Revised by 
24th Annual Supplement (Legal Notice 221 of 2023)
 on 
31 December 2022
]
        
      
    
  






In Pursuance of subsection (2) of section 14 of the Agricultural Finance Corporation Act (Cap. 323), the Treasury determines that the sum of three hundred million shillings shall be the amount of the total indebtedness (whether present or contingent) of the Corporation which shall not be exceeded as the result of the borrowing of any money.
