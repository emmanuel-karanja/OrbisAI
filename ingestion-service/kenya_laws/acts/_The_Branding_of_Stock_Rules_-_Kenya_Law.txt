1.
These Rules may be cited as the Branding of Stock Rules.
2.
Branding-irons and branding instruments shall only be procurable through the Registrar of Brands, c/o the Veterinary Department, P.O. Kabete, Nairobi, to whom all applications shall be addressed.
3.
The persons named in the Schedule to these Rules are authorised to manufacture branding-irons and branding instruments for sale at the respective prices specified in the said Schedule.
