LAWS OF KENYA




ANIMAL DISEASES ACT


THE ANIMAL DISEASES ACT — (FEES AND CHARGES)


LEGAL NOTICE 329 OF 1966





      
        
          
            Published in Kenya Gazette Vol. LXVIII—No. 56 on 29 November 1966
          
        
      
    



          Commenced on 1 February 1966
          
        







    
      
      
        
          [Revised by 
24th Annual Supplement (Legal Notice 221 of 2023)
 on 
31 December 2022
]
        
      
    
  






1.
The fees specified in the Schedule are prescribed in respect of all cattle moved in the Lamu District.
2.
Cattle sold through Lamu stock auction at Mkowe are exempted from the fees.
3.
The fees shall be paid at the Veterinary Station at Lamu before issue of movement permit, except in the case of cattle being moved for sale at the abattoir of the Kenya Meat Commission at Mombasa, fees may, with the consent of a Veterinary Inspector and subject to such other directions as he may give, be paid at the abattoir of the Kenya Meat Commission at Mombasa. In all cases fees shall be paid in respect of the numbers of cattle for which each movement permit is issued.
Schedule
Fees for General Services:
 
Special inspection per head .................................................
4.00
Grazing charge per head ......................................................
1.00
