LAWS OF KENYA




AIR PASSENGER SERVICE CHARGE ACT


THE AIR PASSENGER SERVICE CHARGE (APPORTIONMENT) ORDER 2018


LEGAL NOTICE 23 OF 2019





      
        
          
            Published in Kenya Gazette Vol. CXXIV—No. 47 on 18 April 2019
          
        
      
    



          Commenced on 18 April 2019
          
        







    
      
      
        
          [Revised by 
24th Annual Supplement (Legal Notice 221 of 2023)
 on 
31 December 2022
]
        
      
    
  






1.
This Order may be referred to as the Air Passenger Service Charge (Apportionment) Order, 2018.
2.
The proceeds of the charge collected under section 3(1) of the Act shall be apportioned among the Kenya Airports Authority, the Kenya Civil Aviation Authority and the Tourism Promotion Fund as follows—
(a)
in the case of the charge collected under paragraph (a)—
(i)
sixty percent thereof to the Kenya Airports Authority,
(ii)
twenty percent to the Kenya Civil Aviation Authority; and
(iii)
twenty percent to the Tourism Promotion Fund; and
(b)
in the case of the charge collected under paragraph (b)—
(i)
fifty percent thereof to the Kenya Airports Authority;
(ii)
thirty percent to the Kenya Civil Aviation Authority, and
(iii)
twenty percent to the Tourism Promotion Fund.
