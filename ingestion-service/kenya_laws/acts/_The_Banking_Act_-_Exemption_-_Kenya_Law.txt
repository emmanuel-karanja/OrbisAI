LAWS OF KENYA




BANKING ACT


THE BANKING ACT - EXEMPTION


LEGAL NOTICE 154 OF 2017





      
        
          
            Published in Kenya Gazette Vol. CXIX—No. 109 on 4 August 2017
          
        
      
    



          Commenced on 28 July 2017
          
        







    
      
      
        
          [Revised by 
24th Annual Supplement (Legal Notice 221 of 2023)
 on 
31 December 2022
]
        
      
    
  






IN EXERCISE of the powers conferred by section 53(1) of the Banking Act, the Cabinet Secretary for the National Treasury exempts DIB Bank Kenya Limited from the provisions of section 12 (a) and (c) of the Banking Act.
