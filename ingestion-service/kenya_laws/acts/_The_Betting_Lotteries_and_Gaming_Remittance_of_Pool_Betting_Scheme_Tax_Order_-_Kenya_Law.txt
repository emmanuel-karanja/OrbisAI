LAWS OF KENYA




BETTING, LOTTERIES AND GAMING ACT


THE BETTING, LOTTERIES AND GAMING (REMITTANCE OF POOL BETTING SCHEME TAX) ORDER


LEGAL NOTICE 308 OF 1966





      
        
          
            Published in Kenya Gazette Vol. LXVIII—No. 52 on 1 November 1966
          
        
      
    



          Commenced on 1 January 1967
          
        







    
      
      
        
          [Revised by 
24th Annual Supplement (Legal Notice 221 of 2023)
 on 
31 December 2022
]
        
      
    
  






1.
This Order may be cited as the Betting, Lotteries and Gaming (Remittance of Pool Betting Scheme Tax) Order, and shall come into operation on the 1st January, 1967.
2.
The Permanent Secretary to the Ministry of Home Affairs shall remit directly to the Governments of Tanzania and Uganda (in which countries the Cabinet Secretary is satisfied that arrangements of a reciprocal nature or effect have been made by the legislatures thereof), and not pay into the Consolidated Fund, the sums collected by way of tax on bets made by way of pool betting received in Kenya from those countries respectively, after deduction of the cost of such collection.
