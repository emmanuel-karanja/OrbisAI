LAWS OF KENYA




BOOKS AND NEWSPAPERS ACT


CLASSES OF BOOKS AND NEWSPAPERS EXCLUDED


LEGAL NOTICE 329 OF 1960





      
        
          
            Published in Kenya Gazette Vol. LXII—No. 36 on 19 July 1960
          
        
      
    



          Commenced on 19 July 1960
          
        







    
      
      
        
          [Amended by 
The Books and Newspapers Act-Exclusion (Legal Notice 528 of 1960)
 on 
8 November 1960
]
        
      
    
  



    
      
      
        
          [Amended by 
Books and Newspapers Act-Exclusion (Legal Notice 336 of 1963)
 on 
4 June 1963
]
        
      
    
  



    
      
      
        
          [Amended by 
The Books and Newspapers Act-Exclusion (Legal Notice 144 of 1971)
 on 
9 July 1971
]
        
      
    
  



    
      
      
        
          [Revised by 
24th Annual Supplement (Legal Notice 221 of 2023)
 on 
31 December 2022
]
        
      
    
  






All books and newspapers published—
(a)
by or on behalf of any employer for distribution solely amongst members of his staff;
(b)
by or on behalf of any school registered by the Director of Education under the Basic Education Act (
Cap. 211
), for distribution solely amongst pupils of that school, whether past or present, the parents of such pupils, the governors and staff and persons associated with the school’s activities as benefactors, and the libraries of other schools so registered;
(c)
by or on behalf of any society exempted from registration under the Societies Act (
Cap. 108
), for distribution solely amongst members of that society;
(d)
by or on behalf of any Commonwealth commission or foreign consulate.
