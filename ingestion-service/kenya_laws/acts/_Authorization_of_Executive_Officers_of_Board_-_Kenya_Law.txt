LAWS OF KENYA




BETTING, LOTTERIES AND GAMING ACT


AUTHORIZATION OF EXECUTIVE OFFICERS OF BOARD


LEGAL NOTICE 169 OF 1967





          Commenced on 1 January 1967
          
        







    
      
      
        
          [Amended by 
The Betting, Lotteries and Gaming Act - Authorization of Executive Officers of Board, 2005 (Legal Notice 81 of 2005)
 on 
22 July 2005
]
        
      
    
  



    
      
      
        
          [Revised by 
24th Annual Supplement (Legal Notice 221 of 2023)
 on 
31 December 2022
]
        
      
    
  






IN EXERCISE of the powers conferred by section 3(10) of the Betting, Lotteries and Gaming Act (Cap. 131), the Chairman of the Betting Control and Licensing Board authorizes the Director of Gaming, who is also the Secretary of the Board to issue under his hand all permits and licences issued under the Act and all communications issued from the said Board.
