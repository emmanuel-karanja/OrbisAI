LAWS OF KENYA




BOOKS AND NEWSPAPERS ACT


DIRECTIONS UNDER RULES 2 AND 3 OF THE BOOKS AND NEWSPAPERS RULES


LEGAL NOTICE 386 OF 1962





      
        
          
            Published in Kenya Gazette Vol. LXIV—No. 32 on 31 July 1962
          
        
      
    



          Commenced on 31 July 1962
          
        







    
      
      
        
          [Revised by 
24th Annual Supplement (Legal Notice 221 of 2023)
 on 
31 December 2022
]
        
      
    
  






The Cabinet Secretary has directed that the second copy of each of the books referred to in rule 2 and the second copy of each newspaper and supplement thereto referred to in rule 3 shall be disposed of by the Registrar delivering them to the librarian of the University of Nairobi.
