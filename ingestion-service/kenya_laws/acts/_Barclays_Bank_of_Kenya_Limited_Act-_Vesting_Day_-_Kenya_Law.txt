LAWS OF KENYA




BARCLAYS BANK OF KENYA LIMITED ACT


BARCLAYS BANK OF KENYA LIMITED ACT- VESTING DAY


LEGAL NOTICE 189 OF 1978





      
        
          
            Published in Kenya Gazette Vol. LXXX—No. 39 on 29 September 1978
          
        
      
    



          Commenced on 29 September 1978
          
        






IN EXERCISE of the powers conferred by section 3(1) of the Barclays Bank of Kenya Limited Act, 1978, the Minister for Finance and Planning appoints the 2nd October, 1978, as the vesting day within the meaning of that subsection.
