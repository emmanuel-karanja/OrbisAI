LAWS OF KENYA




ASIAN OFFICERS’ FAMILY PENSIONS ACT


THE ASIAN OFFICERS' FAMILY PENSIONS ACT NOTICE


LEGAL NOTICE 614 OF 1963





      
        
          
            Published in Kenya Gazette Vol. LXV—No. 41 on 10 September 1963
          
        
      
    



          Commenced on 1 October 1963
          
        






CLASSES OF PERSONS DECLARED TO BE INELIGIBLE TO CONTRIBUTE UNDER SECTION 13(1)(G) AND (H)
[L.N. 614/1963, L.N. 667/1963.]
Any Asian officer appointed on or after the 1st October, 1963, under a written agreement expressed to continue for a specified period or periods.
Any Asian officer appointed to the service of the East African Common Service Organization on or after the 1st November, 1963, who immediately before his appointment is not a member of the Asian Officers’ Family Pensions Fund.
