Nofiable Diseases Under Section 2
IN EXERCISE of the powers conferred by section 2 of the Animal Diseases Act, the Minister for Agriculture and Animal Husbandry hereby declares the following to be notifiable diseases for the purposes of the Act
Mucosal Disease Virus Diarrhoea Complex
[
L.N. 147/1965
]
Psittacosis or Ornithosis.
[
L.N. 328/1966
]
Avian Encephalomyelitis (Epidemic Tremor)
[
L.N. 56/1977
]
